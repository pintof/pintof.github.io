Thailand By Sea

Under The Sea

Thailand boasting it's 2,130 KM2 of coral reefs is teaming with life beneath it's seas. It is truly another world down there, for the willing diver to explore. This world is inhabited by a vast range of diverse species. Giant migratory marine species like manta rays, whales, and whale sharks. Intricately patterned, colourful creatures called Nudibranchs that are small as a fingernail. Octopus & cuttlefish that not only look out of this world, but also have matching alien like abilities such as colour changing and light emitting. And gender progressive critters such as Clownfish who can change their gender, and Seahorses where the male takes up the mantle of childbirth.

Above The Sea

Thailand lies smack dab in the middle of two oceans, it's east coast on the Gulf of Thailand is part of the largest Ocean - The Pacific, and it's west coast on the Andaman Sea is part of the smallest Ocean - The Indian. This coastal country offers the seafarer over 500 islands to sail and explore, all being of world class tropical beauty, so much so that it is often featured in Hollywood movies such as 'The Beach' ft. Leonardo DiCaprio. In addition to it's picturesque beauty, the islands also have various species of animals to observe, and sometimes even interact with such as monkeys, monitors, giant snakes, tropical birds, and even tropical girls! The Islands also boast eco-tourism, wild full moon parties, and top notch rock climbing via it's limestone cliffs.
